The Inflation Target


The inflation target is the level of inflation that must be achieved by Bank Indonesia in coordination with the Government. Under the Bank Indonesia Law, the inflation target is established by the Government. In a Memorandum of Understanding between the Government and Bank Indonesia, the inflation target is established for three year period in a Decree of the Minister of Finance (KMK). In PMK No.66/PMK.011/2012, the inflation targets established by the Government for 2013, 2014 and 2015 are 4.5%, 4.5% and 4% with Â±1% deviation.
