Corruption Index

Indonesia scored 36 points out of 100 on the 2015 Corruption Perceptions Index reported by Transparency International. Corruption Index in Indonesia averaged 24.72 Points from 1995 until 2015, reaching an all time high of 36 Points in 2015 and a record low of 17 Points in 1999. Corruption Index in Indonesia is reported by the Transparency International.
