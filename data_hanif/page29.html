SCQ
Situation
Ford one of the largest car producers in the world, a family owned company, Americans, capitalist, don’t want to be owned by the government and don’t want to lose control Huge crisis going on un the world. Government giving money for bail out. Ford decided not to take the money. We have the best product line and we are feeling pretty good about that. We have cars to perfectly match the market, that is, small cars in the pipeline. Gave myself a 30% salary cut, in talks with UAW to reduce salaries and wages, working with suppliers to keep them vibrant and alive, not investing in new capacities, reduces hours of working and also closed some factories Never been in this type of recession not at least in my life, every industry is down. I am losing my sleep. Consumer confidence is all time low and sales have declined 42% We are running through cash from $5 billion to $10 billion every month.
Situation
Will the sales continue to decline? Supplier base may go away Will our product line satisfy current demand? Credit line may be at risk because of the rate at which we are running out of cash
Key Question
How can Ford motors survive through these tough times?
Key Issue
Will Ford motors survive through tough times?
