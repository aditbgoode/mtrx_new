Business Confidence

Business confidence in Indonesia rose to 110.24 in the second quarter of 2016 from 99.46 in the previous three months, marking the highest reading since the third quarter of 2009. All components expanded: income (114.70), production capacity (113.09) and average working hours (104.95). In the June quarter 2015, business confidence came in at 106.0 Business Confidence in Indonesia averaged 106.57 from 2000 until 2016, reaching an all time high of 122.50 in the second quarter of 2000 and a record low of 95.12 in the first quarter of 2006. Business Confidence in Indonesia is reported by the Statistics Indonesia.
