Market Segments
Indonesia SOEs play a major role in the economy. In terms of their number, there are 119 SOEs as of November 2015, 26 of which contributed more than 90 percent of total SOE profit. SOEs are present in almost all sectors/industries. By the end of 2014, SOEs employ around 781,760 people and contribute an estimated 40 percent of the country's GDP. Given the massive stake SOEs have in the economy, the GOI must monitor the performance of its SOEs closely in order to fulfill its fiduciary responsibilities to the country. That's where Matreex provides platform for the Government of Indonesia and its SOEs to better communicate and collaborate to fulfill its fiduciary responsibility
